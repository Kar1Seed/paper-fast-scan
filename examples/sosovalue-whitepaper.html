一、什么是 SoSoValue

SoSoValue is an AI-powered crypto investment and research platform that aims to enable crypto investment to the global masses. The ecosystem comprises four major pillars: SoSoValue Terminal — a comprehensive crypto market research hub that synthesizes vast streams of data into actionable insights; SoSoValue Indexes (SSI) Protocol — an on-chain spot index protocol on EVM for transparent passive portfolios; SoDEX — a high-performance decentralized order book exchange; and ValueChain — a dedicated Layer-1 blockchain built for SoDEX and the wider ecosystem. By integrating data, AI, index protocols, and next-gen trading infrastructure, SoSoValue provides a complete investment stack from information discovery to portfolio construction and professional trading.
SoSoValue 是一个AI 驱动的加密投资研究平台，致力于让全球大众都能参与加密投资。生态由四大支柱组成：SoSoValue Terminal——综合加密市场研究枢纽，将海量数据提炼为可执行的洞察；SSI 指数协议——基于 EVM 的链上现货指数协议，实现透明的被动投资组合；SoDEX——高性能去中心化订单簿交易所；ValueChain——专为 SoDEX 及更广泛生态构建的 Layer-1 区块链。通过整合数据、AI、指数协议和下一代交易基础设施，SoSoValue 提供从信息发现到组合构建再到专业交易的完整投资栈。

二、市场三大痛点

Three core issues face today's crypto investors: 1. Information Gap — information is fragmented across different circles with no effective integration, making it difficult to form well-informed decisions. 2. Absence of Simple Passive Investing — unlike traditional finance where index funds provide simple market exposure, crypto lacks an easy and trustworthy way to gain broad market growth. 3. Inefficient On-Chain Infrastructure — AMM-based DEXs suffer from slippage and capital inefficiency; liquidity is shallow and fragmented; cross-chain operations are cumbersome. In crypto, a rational investment decision requires at least 10 different tools. The most fundamental elements — information, investment products, and trading infrastructure — should be clean, transparent, and accessible.
当今加密投资者面临三大核心问题：1. 信息鸿沟——信息在不同圈子中碎片化分布，缺乏有效整合，难以形成全面判断。2. 缺乏简单可靠的被动投资——传统金融有指数基金提供简单市场敞口，加密市场却缺少类似工具。3. 链上基础设施低效——基于 AMM 的 DEX 存在滑点和资金效率问题；流动性浅且碎片化；跨链操作繁琐。在加密世界做一次理性投资决策，至少需要 10 种不同工具。最基础的三要素——信息、投资产品、交易基础设施——本应像水和空气一样清洁透明。

SoSoValue 对全网数据进行了重新分类和重组，从投资视角建立了七大板块：加密市场总览（5 个子仪表盘：二级市场、加密股票、一级融资、ETF、DEX 池）；板块与指数（从 Layer1 到应用的 13 个主要板块）；代币与项目（代币经济学、解锁计划、团队信息）；新闻与研究（AI 聚合新闻、深度研报、专家观点）；宏观经济（政策变化、全球冲突、传统市场动态）；链上数据（区块链健康指标）；以及 Token Bar——AI 跨语言翻译的协作研究社区。

3.3 Protocol — 协议让投资可靠

The SSI Protocol leverages on-chain smart contracts to repackage multi-chain, multi-asset portfolios into Wrapped Tokens (SSI). These tokens represent a basket of underlying assets, enabling passive index investing — tracking the value of the underlying spot basket. The protocol eliminates traditional barriers like high fees, slow settlement, and regional restrictions. Six participant types form the ecosystem: User (investors), UNI LP (Uniswap liquidity providers), WLP (whitelist participants who arbitrage between Uniswap and NAV), PMM (private market makers providing depth), Keeper (automated rebalancing and maintenance), and Index Council (governance over index methodology).
SSI 协议利用链上智能合约将多链、多资产组合重新打包为封装代币 (SSI)。这些代币代表一篮子底层资产，实现被动指数投资——跟踪底层现货篮子的价值波动。协议消除了高费用、慢结算、地域限制等传统障碍。六类参与者构成生态：用户（投资者）、UNI LP（Uniswap 流动性提供者）、WLP（白名单参与者，在 Uniswap 和净值间套利）、PMM（私有做市商提供深度）、Keeper（自动化再平衡和维护）、指数委员会（治理指数方法论）。

3.4 Infrastructure — 基础设施让生态可持续

For crypto investment to truly scale, investors need a sustainable underlying infrastructure. This is delivered by SoDEX — a high-performance, fully on-chain order book DEX eliminating AMM slippage — and ValueChain — a purpose-built Layer-1 with HostChain + GuestChain architecture allowing spot, perps, RWA, and other appchains to interoperate while sharing security and liquidity. This modular yet unified design ensures that trading, settlement, and risk management are transparent, verifiable, and censorship-resistant.
加密投资要真正规模化，需要可持续的底层基础设施。这由SoDEX——高性能全链上订单簿 DEX，消除 AMM 滑点——和ValueChain——采用HostChain + GuestChain 架构的专用 Layer-1，让现货、合约、RWA 等应用链共享安全性和流动性——共同交付。这种模块化而统一的设计确保交易、结算和风险管理透明、可验证、抗审查。

Six roles form the SSI Protocol ecosystem: User — purchases index tokens for market beta/alpha. UNI LP — provides liquidity on Uniswap, earns trading fees. 六类角色构成 SSI 协议生态：用户——购买指数代币获取市场 beta/alpha。UNI LP——在 Uniswap 提供流动性赚手续费。WLP（白名单参与者）——通过协议铸造/赎回 SSI，利用资产净值 (NAV) 在 Uniswap 套利维持价格锚定。PMM（私有做市商）——提供深度流动性和窄价差。Keeper——自动化机器人处理再平衡、NAV 更新和系统维护。指数委员会——去中心化治理指数方法论、纳入标准和再平衡规则。

5.4 指数编制方法论

The SSI index methodology follows a standardized framework: Eligibility — tokens must trade on qualified exchanges (Binance, OKX, Coinbase, Bybit, Bitget), be supported by SoSoValue data, and meet liquidity and market cap thresholds. Weighting — constituents are weighted by a combination of market capitalization and liquidity metrics with caps on individual weight to ensure diversification. Rebalancing — indices are periodically rebalanced (monthly/quarterly) with buffer rules to minimize unnecessary turnover. The methodology is open-source and governed by the Index Council.
SSI 指数方法论遵循标准化框架：纳入资格——代币必须在合格交易所（Binance、OKX、Coinbase、Bybit、Bitget）交易、获 SoSoValue 数据支持、满足流动性和市值门槛。加权方式——成分按市值和流动性指标组合加权，设单成分权重上限确保分散化。再平衡——指数定期再平衡（月/季），设缓冲区规则减少不必要换手。方法论开源，由指数委员会治理。

6.3 跨链架构

SOSO was originally deployed as an ERC-20 token on Ethereum, then bridged to Base. Following the ValueChain mainnet launch, SOSO is designated as the native token of ValueChain. The cross-chain mechanism uses Lock-and-Release (not Lock-and-Mint): on the source chain (Ethereum/Base), ERC-20 SOSO is permanently locked in the bridge contract; on ValueChain, an equivalent amount of native SOSO is released from the bridge reserve. This ensures every unit on ValueChain is fully 1:1 backed by locked ERC-20 SOSO, maintaining a constant total circulating supply.
SOSO 最初在以太坊上以 ERC-20 发行，后通过官方桥扩展到 Base。ValueChain 主网上线后，SOSO 被指定为 ValueChain 的原生代币。跨链采用 Lock-and-Release（锁定释放）机制而非 Lock-and-Mint：源链（以太坊/Base）上的 ERC-20 SOSO 被永久锁定在桥合约中；ValueChain 上从桥储备释放等量原生 SOSO。这确保 ValueChain 上的每个 SOSO 都由锁定的 ERC-20 SOSO 1:1 全额背书，总流通量恒定。
以太坊/Base: ERC-20 SOSO → 锁定在桥合约
ValueChain: 等量原生 SOSO ← 从桥储备释放
总流通量: 恒定不变（锁定量 = 释放量）
